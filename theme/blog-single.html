Coffee Bean Sources

Coffee seeds are harvested by our partner farmers. Coffee cherries are picked and processed to extract the beans. A team member sources coffee at the site. Beans of the same size and shape are placed in the roaster to ensure even roasting. While roasting, the beans are watched closely to ensure the perfect roast, which is based on color and texture. Once the preferred roast is achieved, the beans are moved to a cooling tray to stop the roasting process. After cooling, the beans are packaged to seal in flavor and aroma.
